Who We are

About Us

Since its founding in 2019, Uruvam Fitness Zone has been recognized as a leading authority in the fitness industry. UFZ serves long-standing commitment to its core idea weight-lifting tradition.
Today, Uruvam Fitness Zone has expanded its fitness profile to offer varied latest equipment and services, including group exercise, personal training, cardiovascular equipment, spinning, and Crossfit training.

The difference UFZ adds to its clients makes it a people's choice all over the world from fitness enthusiasts to professional athletes, bodybuilders, including members of the armed forces. Each client is unique and so will be their eating and activity strategy that we meticulously craft to evoke fully their individual potential.

Mr. R. Vijayakumar M.E,Ph.D. is a licensed Personal trainer and a certified Nutritionist from respected authorities. He has been in the fitness industry for over two decades. Over the course of the previous 15+ years, he has established himself as an expert in the fields of fitness, bodybuilding, weight lifting, and powerlifting. He had competed in a large number of bodybuilding and powerlifting competitions in and around Tamil Nadu, where he won both gold and silver medals.

As a motivational speaker, he has assisted a great number of students in breaking Guinness records and achieving their fitness goals.He is also a motivational speaker and guest speaker where he visited more than twenty institutions and schools.Still, as a professor at a prestigious institution where he has worked for the past 10+ years, he is contributing to healthy society by shaping the behaviour of students in a positive way. He is responsible for the success of over 700 and more engineers around the world, and yet his service is still ongoing... He submitted patent applications for more than ten different inventions in the engineering and fitness industries, and he published more than six different works. In the fields of engineering, social, fitness, and nutrition, he contributed to more than 20 national and international journals' publications. In addition to that, he is conducting research in a variety of fields.

Uruvam Fitness Zone, is a premium fitness gym at Veerapandi Pirivu, Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, conceptualized by Mr.R.Vijayakumar, B.E., M.E, (Ph.D), young fitness enthusiast and body builder from Coimbatore.
